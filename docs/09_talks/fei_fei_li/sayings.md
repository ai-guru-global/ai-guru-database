# 李飞飞 (Fei-Fei Li) 关于 AI 的观点与格言

> 说明：以下整理李飞飞教授在公开场合关于 AI 的代表性言论，提供中英文对照与出处链接。

## 1. 人本与伦理 (Human-Centered & Ethics)

1. **“AI is about people, not machines.” / “AI 关乎人，而不只是机器。”**  
   - **上下文**: 强调以人为本的 AI 设计理念。  
   - **来源**: [Fei-Fei Li @ Stanford HAI Launch Keynote, 2019](https://hai.stanford.edu/news/stanford-hai-inaugural-conference)

2. **“There’s nothing artificial about AI. It’s inspired by people, intended to benefit people, and it has to be guided by human concerns.” / “AI 没有什么‘人工’可言，它源于人、服务人、必须由人类价值引导。”**  
   - **上下文**: 强调人类价值与责任。  
   - **来源**: [Fei-Fei Li TED Talk, 2017](https://www.ted.com/talks/fei_fei_li_how_we_teach_computers_to_understand_pictures)

## 2. 数据与偏见 (Data & Bias)

3. **“The future of AI needs diversity.” / “AI 的未来需要多样性。”**  
   - **上下文**: 呼吁多样化数据与团队，降低偏见。  
   - **来源**: [MIT Technology Review Interview, 2018](https://www.technologyreview.com/2018/03/20/144979/ai-is-getting-powerful-but-its-biased/)

## 3. 产业与社会影响 (Industry & Impact)

4. **“We are seeing a Cambrian explosion in AI applications.” / “AI 应用正经历寒武纪大爆发。”**  
   - **上下文**: 描述行业应用的指数级增长。  
   - **来源**: [Stanford AI Index Report Briefing, 2021](https://aiindex.stanford.edu/)
